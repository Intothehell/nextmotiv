HATTAT TRAKTÖR

A division of the esteemed Hattat Group

Hattat Traktör is a leading Turkish tractor manufacturer renowned for combining robust local engineering with a global vision. Operating from a massive, high-tech facility near Istanbul, Hattat produces a comprehensive range of tractors from 50 to 122 horsepower.

Distinguished by in-house manufacturing of critical components like transmissions and axles. While proudly producing Valtra A-series models for worldwide distribution, the Hattat brand has forged its own path.

Exported to over 80 countries and earning multiple "Tractor of the Year" awards. With continued innovation—evidenced by the new T4000 series with advanced Stage V engines—Hattat delivers cutting-edge technology to empower farmers around the world.
