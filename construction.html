Kubota has established itself as a global leader in the compact construction equipment sector, securing the world's largest market share in mini excavators (Off-Highway Research 2024) and ranking among the top global manufacturers. Renowned in Europe, North America, and Asia for their advanced engineering and operator-focused amenities, Kubota's expanding lineup is designed to meet the world's most rigorous safety and emission standards.

In India, Kubota is executing an ambitious growth strategy through its joint venture Escorts Kubota, positioning the country as a central pillar of its global 2030 vision by transforming it into a "growth engine" and global hub for R&D, procurement, and production. This includes launching new BS V-compliant construction equipment, unveiling application-specific models like the U22-6 mini excavator and mining cranes at Exxon 2025, and establishing a massive new ₹4,500 crore manufacturing plant in Uttar Pradesh.

Backed by a strategically optimized global production framework and a $3.23 billion R&D buildup targeting India and North America, Kubota is committed to rapidly responding to evolving market demands while broadening its presence in the material handling industry to deliver reliable, high-performance solutions for every job site.
