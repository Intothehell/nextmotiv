INDUSTRIAL & AGRICULTURAL TYRES

OTR Tyres

OTR tyres are defined by several core characteristics: deep, specialized tread patterns (from ribbed for hard surfaces to open bars for soft soil) that provide traction and self-cleaning; reinforced sidewalls and cut-resistant rubber compounds to withstand punctures, abrasion, and impacts from debris; high load capacity to support heavy machinery at relatively low speeds (typically 10–30 mph); and flexible or stiff construction depending on application—flexible for agricultural use to reduce soil compaction, stiff for industrial use to enhance stability on hard ground. These features allow OTR tyres to perform reliably in extreme off-highway conditions.
