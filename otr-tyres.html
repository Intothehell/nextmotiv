OTR Tyres

OTR tyres are defined by several core characteristics: deep, specialized tread patterns that provide traction and self-cleaning; reinforced sidewalls and cut-resistant rubber compounds to withstand punctures and impacts; high load capacity to support heavy machinery; and flexible or stiff construction depending on application. These features allow OTR tyres to perform reliably in extreme off-highway conditions.
